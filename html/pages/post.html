11

I often want to find some peace in the chaos, but it is not easy. It is so bizarre at the moment, and my heart is so messy. When a person has only memories left, his career is probably boring, but sometimes there are no memories. China’s fuss is on track, and things in the world are still spiraling. When I left Sun Yat-Sen University a few days ago, I remembered leaving Xiamen University four months ago. When I heard the plane tweet over my head, I remembered the plane that was flying around Beijing a year ago. I also made a short article called "Yijue". Now it is, even this "sense" is gone.

The weather in Guangzhou was really hot early, and the setting sun shot in from the west window, forcing people to barely wear a single shirt. The pot of "water horizontal branches" on the desk is something I haven't seen before: it's just a section of tree. As long as it is immersed in water, the branches and leaves will be lovely. Look at Luye, compile old manuscripts, and finally do something. Doing these things can really get rid of the heat in the day of life and the year of death.

The day before yesterday, the "Weeds" had been compiled; this time it was the turn of "Revisiting the Past" on "Mangyuan", and I also changed the name for him: "Crossing Flowers in the Evening". With dew flowers, the color and fragrance are naturally much better, but I can't. Even the bizarre and miscellaneous in my mind now, I still can't make him turn into a bizarre and miscellaneous article immediately. Or, when he looks up at the flowing clouds every day, it will flash in front of my eyes.

For a while, I have repeatedly recalled the fruits and vegetables I ate in my hometown when I was a child: water chestnut, Luo Handou, wild water chestnut, and cantaloupe. All of these are extremely delicious and delicious; they were all fascinations that made me homesick. Later, I tasted it after a long absence, and it was nothing more than that; only in memory, the old meaning remained. They may want to deceive me for life and make me look back from time to time.
